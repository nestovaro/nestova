Our Operational Framework

How Nestova Works Only With Developers You Can Trust

We have built four independent internal units that ensure every project on our platform has been scrutinised before it reaches any client. No approval means no listing — no exceptions.

Submit a Developer for Review
a
Developer Verification Framework (Mandatory)

Every developer must pass our five-point verification before a single square metre is listed.

  • Corporate registration and ownership verification
  • Track record of completed and delivered projects
  • Site inspection and construction stage validation
  • Title and legal documentation review
  • Delivery timeline and construction capacity assessment
No approval → No listing. No exceptions.
b
Project Due-Diligence Desk

An internal unit that operates independently from our sales team, responsible for:

  • Title authenticity checks
  • Survey and layout review
  • Planning approvals verification
  • Project risk profiling
c
Structured Listing Approval Process

No agent or marketer can upload or promote a project unless it is:

  • Officially cleared by our compliance desk
  • Assigned a Nestova Project ID
  • Backed by verified documentation on file
d
Post-Sales & Client Protection Unit

Nestova does not disappear after payment. Our client protection unit follows up with:

  • Ongoing construction updates and milestone reporting
  • Delivery timeline accountability with developers
  • Escalation channels for any client concern
  • Dedicated client support desk from purchase to handover
